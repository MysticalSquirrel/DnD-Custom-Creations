# **Quest Master's Guide** Version **0.0.0**

## **Masteries:**
- **Cursed**:
  - Your Proficiency Bonus is doubled and applied negatively.

- **Proficient**:
  - Your Proficiency Bonus is doubled.

- **Expert**:
  - Your Proficiency Bonus is tripled.

- **Master**:
  - Your Proficiency Bonus is quadrupled.
